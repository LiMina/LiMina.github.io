Year Three

Spring 2015

Software Engineering
Computer Science 169 | Armando Fox
In this class, our main focus was to work on an application with a non-profit organization using RoR on an Agile development cycle.
Introduction to Database Systems
Computer Science 186 | Joe Hellerstein
The course title is fairly self-explanatory, but we learned about databases from the low-level details all the way up to query optimization in PostgreSQL.
Introduction to Science, Technology and Society (STS)
History C182C | Massimo Mazzotti and David Winickoff
This class explored the intersectionality of science in history and philosophy, and examined the ethical and moral dilemmas that scientists should be aware of.
Pros and Con(dom)s: The Science of Birth Control
Integrated Biology 198 | Phoebe Chen and Parinaz Jafarieh (students)
A student-taught class that discussed the science behind currently available birth control methods, examined future possibilities of birth control, and debunked various sex myths.

Fall 2014

Musical Applications of Computers and Related Technologies
Music 158 | Ilya Y Rostovtsev
This class is, for all intents and purposes, a computer music class. We worked with Max/MSP, which allowed us to play with frequencies and filters, and manipulating them with the mouse, keyboard, and Arduino Esploras. This was taken as a technical elective for the CS major.
Efficient Algorithms and Intractable Problems
Computer Science 170 | David Wagner
One of the most difficult, but rewarding classes I took during my time in undergrad, this class is considered to be an introduction to CS theory.
Computer Security
Computer Science 161 | Doug Tygar
As someone who has worked in information security, it seemed like a shame if I didn't take this class. The class was separated out into three section: application security, network security, and operating system security.
Celtic Mythology
Celtic C168 | Annalee Rejhon
This class went into pre-Christian beliefs of the Celtic and Indo-European worlds, covering texts such as The Tain, and The Mabinogi.
