Cinnamon

She is a Columbian Rainbow Boa (Epicrates cenchria maurus). She's only a baby right now, and is somewhere between 18 to 24 inches, but when she's an adult, she'll be somewhere between 3 to 5 feet long. She'll also grow more red in color, and her rainbow irridescence will be more obvious in the light.

We feed her mice that are about the same size as the widest part of her body.

If you're wondering, no, we keep him in her own tank and not with the geckos.
